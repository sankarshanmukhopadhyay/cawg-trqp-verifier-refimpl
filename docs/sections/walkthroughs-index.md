# Walkthroughs

Worked, end-to-end examples that show the full decision lifecycle — request,
policy evaluation, decision receipt, and replay — applied to a realistic
scenario. These are written to be readable by non-specialists as well as
implementers.
## Available walkthroughs

- [Photography Contest Verification](../workflows/photography-contest-verification.md) shows governed eligibility, signed rule feeds, revocation, receipts, and appeals.
- [Video Verification Walkthrough](../video-verification-walkthrough.md) explains the generic video-verification pipeline for an official workflow.
- [Newsroom Citizen-Video Verification](../workflows/newsroom-citizen-video-verification.md) separates provenance, TRQP policy evaluation, editorial corroboration, and accountable broadcast approval.
