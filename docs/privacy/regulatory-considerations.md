# Regulatory Considerations

This page is an implementation checklist, not legal advice or a compliance statement.

## European Union

Assess personal-data status, purpose and lawful basis, transparency, controller and processor roles, minimization, retention, rights, security, automated decision effects, international transfers, and whether a data-protection impact assessment is required.

## India

Assess the applicable Data Fiduciary and processor roles, notice and specified purpose, consent or other permitted processing grounds, security safeguards, breach response, correction and erasure, grievance handling, children's data, and any notified transfer conditions.

## United States

Assess applicable state privacy laws, sectoral requirements, consumer-protection representations, security expectations, children's privacy, biometric or voice rules, publicity rights, and contractual obligations. Requirements vary materially by state, sector, and use case.

## Deployment evidence

A deployment should produce:

- approved processing-purpose profile;
- data inventory;
- role and responsibility matrix;
- context and redaction profiles;
- retention schedule;
- access-control evidence;
- transfer map;
- incident response procedure;
- human-review and correction procedure;
- privacy test report.
